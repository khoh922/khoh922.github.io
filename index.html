About

I am a Moore Foundation Postdoctoral Fellow in Physical Chemistry at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Michael Zuerch. I completed my PhD in Physics at MIT under Prof. Nuh Gedik.

My research focuses on ultrafast dynamics in quantum materials, including charge density waves, superconductors, and nonequilibrium phases of matter, using time-resolved diffraction and spectroscopy techniques.

Research Interests

  • Ultrafast dynamics of charge density waves
  • Time-resolved X-ray scattering and diffraction
  • Ultrafast electron diffraction (UED)
  • Quantum materials and nonequilibrium phases
  • Attosecond XUV spectroscopy
